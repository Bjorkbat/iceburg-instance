About

Iceburg is a game about that part of the internet we don't see. The part that the search giants tend to ignore...or avoid.

Maybe you've already heard about the Deep Web. You've heard about sites where shady people congregate to buy drugs online with cryptocurrency, look at illegal pornography, maybe do something more innocent like trade pirated books on anarchist philosophy.

But what if there's more?

What if the urban legends are true about the Deep Web? What if there are government databases filled with classified material? Or secret hangouts for only the most skilled hackers? What if a secret shadow cabal is building a self-aware AI somewhere in the deep? What would you do if you found it?

This is a game about exploring what might lie below the surface.
